# The Mental Load

A multimodal piece for Critical Making (Spring 2026, Week 14: Combinations).

**Live site:** https://kimberspencer.github.io/mental-load/

## Three ingredients

- **`index.html`** — a Twine-style hypertext hub. Passages reveal on click; interruptions ("MOM!") appear unprompted on a timer.
- **`overflow.html`** — a P5.js sketch where words pile up faster than you can clear them. Builds on the text-click sketch from Exercise Ten.
- **`comic.html`** — a four-panel SVG comic about folding laundry while being called for. The text drops out in the final panel.

## Theme

The mental load of caregiving and grad school. The loop is the form. The loop is the argument.
